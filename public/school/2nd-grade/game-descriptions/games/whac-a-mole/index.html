Whac-A-Mole

by Nina Moritz and Lenard Okorn

Whac-A-Mole is an arcade game where the player hits moles that pop up randomly in holes laid out across the surface of the machine.

In our implementation there will be a grid where a mole pops up randomly every second and the player has to click on it before it disappears. There will be a 60 second timer and points for every mole you whack.
